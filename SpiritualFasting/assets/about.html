Spiritual Fasting is an Android™ application that is designed to inform, ENCOURAGE, and ORGANIZE.

  • INFORM you with the types of spiritual fasting that the Lord speaks of in the Bible.
  • ENCOURAGE you to fast as he expects you to.
  • ORGANIZE your fasting journey.

This application provides information about various types of fasts and it provides a way of keeping track of any fast that you would like to start.
