Disown Sinful Influences

The final step to spiritual freedom involves disowning sinful influences that come from friends and acquaintances. Each of us is predisposed to certain behavior from several sources, including:

  • Emotional/Psychological problems
  • Genetics
  • Direct sinful stimulation
  • Wrong heroes or roles models
  • Direct satanic or demonic activity
For many people, these things are part of their family heritage. To gain spiritual liberty over the bondage associated with these influences, we must disown the sins of others and their influence on our lives.
