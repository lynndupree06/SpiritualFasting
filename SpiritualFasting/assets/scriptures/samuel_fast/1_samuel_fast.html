Call the Body Together

1 Samuel 7:5 KJV And Samuel said, Gather all Israel to Mizpeh, and I will pray for you unto the LORD. The results of individual and corporate fasting are distinctly different from each other. When a person embarks on a fast, he is submitting himself individually to God. Corporate fasting makes a person responsible for what God does for the entire Church. When a Church comes together to fast or when a small group may come together to fast, then God will answer the corporate prayer and fasting effort.
