Two different fasting methods are considered radical fasting. The first is when no food or water are taken, like Jesus did when he fasted in the wilderness. The second form of radical fasting allows water but nothing else. You can find a number of biblical examples of radical fasting. Here are a few:

  • David (2 Samuel 12:15-20)
  • Esther and her family (Esther 4:15-16)
  • Moses (Deuteronomy 9:9-18)
  • Jesus (Matthew 4:1-11)
